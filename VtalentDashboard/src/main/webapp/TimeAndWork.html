1)A, B and C can do a piece of work in 20, 30 and 60 days respectively. In how many days can A do the work if he is assisted by B and C on every third day?

Option B

A's 2 day's work = ( 1/20 * 2 ) = 1/10.

Whole work willl be done in(3*5) = 15 days

2) A can do a certain work in the same time in which B and C together can do it. If A and B together could do it in 10 days and C alone in 50 days, then B alone could do it in?

A's 1 day's work = 3/50

B's 1 day's work (1/10 - 3/50)=2/50= 1/25.

4) A alone can do a piece of work in 6 days and B alone in 8 days. A and B undertook to do it for Rs. 3200. With the help of C, they completed the work in 3 days. How much is to be paid to C?



Explanation:

Option B

5)If 6 men and 8 boys can do a piece of work in 10 days while 26 men and 48 boys can do the same in 2 days, the time taken by 15 men and 20 boys in doing the same type of work will be ?



Explanation:

Option A

Let 1 man's 1 day's work = x and 1 boy's 1 day's work = y

Then, 6x + 8y = 1/10 and 26x + 48y = 1/2.

Solving these two equations, we get : x = 1/100 and y= 1/200.

(15 men + 20 boy)'s 1 day's work =(15/100 + 20/200) = 1/4.

ANS :15 men and 20 boys can do the work in 4 days.
